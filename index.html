Our Story

A True Tralee Local

The Greyhound Bar on Pembroke Street is everything a great Irish pub should be — warm, welcoming, and genuine. Owner Aidan runs the bar with the friendliness and care of someone who truly loves what he does, personally greeting regulars and first-timers alike.

We're a family-run local that takes pride in keeping prices fair, portions generous, and the craic mighty. Whether you're dropping in for a quiet pint, catching the match on the big screen, or sitting down for a hearty lunch — you'll always feel at home here.

No fake leprechauns. No tourist gimmicks. Just good food, good drink, and good company — as it should be.
